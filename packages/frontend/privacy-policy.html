Privacy Policy

Welcome to Jade Dynasty's Privacy Policy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide to us through our website.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information such as your name, email address, shipping address, and payment details when you place an order or contact us. We also collect non-personal information through analytics tools to understand website usage trends, including the number of visits, clicks on certain buttons, and anonymized data such as location and device type.

Use of Information

We use the information we collect to process orders, provide customer support, and improve our website and services based on user feedback. Analytics tools help us analyze website traffic and trends to enhance user experience.

Third-Party Services

We utilise analytics tools to track and analyse website traffic, helping us understand how our website is used and improve our services. Additionally, we use advertising tracking tools to measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to access and update your personal information, request deletion of your information (subject to exceptions), and opt-out of marketing communications from us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Please review this policy periodically for updates.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through the contact form on our website.
