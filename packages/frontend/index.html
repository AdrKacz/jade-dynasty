Our story

Inspired by the rich heritage of Traditional Chinese Medicine, Jade Dynasty was founded by Alex Carter from York, England. Alex discovered his passion for jade carving while living in the picturesque village of Yangshuo, nestled amidst the stunning karst landscapes of Guangxi Province.

There, he forged strong bonds with local artisans who imparted their ancient techniques. Today, we collaborate closely with these skilled craftsmen, preserving centuries-old traditions of transforming raw jade into works of art that harmonize body, mind, and spirit.

Craftmanship and quality

Each Jade Dynasty Gua Sha is meticulously crafted from premium jade sourced from sustainable mines, ensuring the highest quality and authenticity. Our artisans combine traditional methods with modern design, creating tools that not only enhance your skincare routine but also promote holistic wellness through the gentle art of Gua Sha.
